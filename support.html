Popular Questions

How do I recover my account? ▼
To recover your account, please go to the login page and click on "Forgot Password". You will need access to the email address registered to your account to receive the verification code.
Why is my game lagging? ▼
Lag can be caused by unstable internet connections or high server load. Please ensure you are connected to a stable Wi-Fi, close background apps, or try lowering your in-game graphic settings.
How do I report a bug? ▼
You can report a bug directly using the Support Form above. Please select "Report Bug" as the problem type and provide as much detail as possible, including device specs and error codes.
How do I change my account email? ▼
Currently, email addresses linked to a Wuthering Waves account cannot be changed directly for security reasons. Please contact Player Support through the form for further assistance.
